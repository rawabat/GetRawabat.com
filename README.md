## 🧠 What is Rawabat?

Rawabat is a SaaS platform that transforms small and medium businesses into fully automated, AI-driven operations.

We combine:
- 🤖 AI Agents
- 🔁 Workflow Automation
- 💬 WhatsApp Commerce
- 📊 Lightweight CRM

Into one unified system.

---

## 💡 The Problem

SMEs in MENA face:

- Slow response to customers → lost sales
- Manual operations → inefficiency
- Fragmented tools → no central system
- No automation → no scalability

📉 Result: Revenue leakage + operational chaos

---

## ⚡ Our Solution

Rawabat acts as a **Business OS**:

→ Automates customer conversations  
→ Converts chats into orders  
→ Manages operations automatically  
→ Generates insights and reports  

All from one platform.
